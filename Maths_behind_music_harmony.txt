The Mathematics Behind Music and Harmony
1. Introduction

Music and mathematics have been closely connected for thousands of years. Ancient mathematicians such as Pythagoras discovered that musical harmony is governed by simple numerical ratios between sound frequencies.

When two musical notes sound pleasant together (a consonance), their frequencies often form simple ratios such as 2:1 or 3:2. These ratios form the mathematical foundation of musical scales, chords, and harmony.

Problem Statement

The goal of this project is to explore:

The mathematical relationship between frequency ratios and musical harmony

How musical scales are constructed mathematically

How harmony can be simulated and analyzed using Python

Objectives

The project will:

Explain the mathematics behind musical intervals

Implement frequency ratio calculations

Generate musical scales mathematically

Simulate harmonic relationships

Visualize the mathematical structure of music
